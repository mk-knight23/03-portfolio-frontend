About

UI/UX craft &
animation obsession

Frontend Engineer at Wipro, building the AI-VIBE-ECOSYSTEM v2.0 with a focus on motion-first design.

I'm a Frontend Engineer who believes the best interfaces are the ones that feel alive. Every hover state, every page transition, every scroll interaction is an opportunity to create delight.

At Wipro, I bridge design and engineering — translating Figma prototypes into performant React applications with buttery-smooth animations. My toolkit includes GSAP, Framer Motion, CSS animations, and a deep understanding of the Web Animations API.

Currently architecting the AI-VIBE-ECOSYSTEM v2.0, where I'm exploring how AI can enhance — not replace — the craft of frontend development. Motion systems, generative UI patterns, and agentic design tools.

Design Philosophy

Motion should guide, not distract. Every animation needs purpose — whether it's directing attention, providing feedback, or creating emotional connection.
