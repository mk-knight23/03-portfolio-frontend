# Design System: 03-studio-site

## Visual Philosophy
The design follows a **Bold Creative Agency** aesthetic, characterized by high-contrast typography, fluid motion, and immersive interactive highlights.

## Design Elements
-   **Typography**: Bold serif and sans-serif pairings (PP Mori) for a sophisticated editorial feel.
-   **Motion Language**: Staggered entrances, parabolic scrolling effects, and SVG mask transitions.
-   **Adaptive Surfaces**: Dynamic background color shifting based on scroll position (Light/Dark transitions).

## Color Palette
-   **Base**: Jet Black (#030303)
-   **Accent Pulse**: Lavendar/Purple (#EDBFFF)
-   **Neutral High**: Pure White (#FFFFFF)

## Micro-interactions
-   **Sticky Cursor**: Custom cursor that morphs and captures images on hover.
-   **Smooth Navigation**: Staggered link reveals and persistent branding.
-   **Video Immersions**: Responsive video backgrounds that scale based on focus.
